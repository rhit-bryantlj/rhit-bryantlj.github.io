About

I am currently a Junior attending Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology striving to complete a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering with a minor in Computer Science. I have had the opportunity to work with many amazing people at Rose class projects and am ready to continue my work experience through internships. I am passionate about working with different types of technology, especially new cutting edge technologies.

On campus I am apart of the Association of Future Alumni (AFA), CSERVE, and the Pi Kappa Alpha Fraternity (PIKE). For AFA, we reach out to the Rose-Hulman alumni to connect on a professional level and make sure they feel welcome when returning to campus. The group also helps to put on a dinner and get together during homecoming for the alumni. CSERVE is an organized group that supports Career Services to put on the Career Fair. We help the companies setup and cleanup as well as provide help getting anything they need durin the fair. Lastly, PIKE is one of my bigest commitments. I am currently the Vice President of Enrichment. This oversees internal affairs between the brothers, sitting on the Exec board, and interpreting the bylaws.
